About Textile Calculation

Our Mission

At Textile Calculation, our mission is to provide textile professionals, students, and enthusiasts with precise, reliable, and easy-to-use calculation tools that simplify complex textile measurements and conversions.

We understand the challenges faced in textile manufacturing and education when it comes to accurate measurements and calculations. Our goal is to bridge this gap by offering a comprehensive suite of specialized calculators designed specifically for the textile industry.

By providing these tools, we aim to improve efficiency, reduce errors, and enhance the learning experience for everyone involved in the textile sector, from students and teachers to professionals in mills and design studios.

About the Owner

Anil Kamboj

Textile Expert & Jacquard Designer

With over 15 years of experience as a jacquard designer in the textile industry, Anil Kamboj brings a wealth of practical knowledge and expertise to Textile Calculation.

Throughout his career, Anil has worked with numerous textile mills and manufacturers, gaining firsthand insight into the challenges faced by professionals when performing various calculations essential for production planning and quality control.

His passion for simplifying complex textile calculations and making them accessible to everyone in the industry led to the creation of Textile Calculation, a platform he continues to develop and enhance based on industry feedback and evolving needs.
